NETHUNT

-Starve to Solve Mysteries

Are you good at guesswork? Gear up, this is the right spot to test your skills in wordplay. The pictures will tell all that you need but don’t forget to watch out for the nitty-gritty details. Everything matters here. Be ready to vanquish the limits of your imagination and win over this event.

EVENT SPLIT UP

ROUND 1

Net Hunt is an online event. In this event, an image will be displayed to the players and they have to guess the technical term denoted by the image. Clues will be provided for each image after a fixed number of hits (submissions). Once the player guesses the correct word the next image will be displayed.

Rules

• The answers will not contain any special characters or white spaces.

• First clue appears after 50 hits and the second clue appears after 125 hits.

• Scores will be reduced according to the hit counts

• Winners will be announced based on the Leader board.

• Any form of malpractice will lead to disqualification

• Selection will be made after verification of student’s credentials. Students with invalid credentials will be disqualified.
